## Sheet 1: ChatGPT Power User Reference

### Top 10 Prompt Formulas

1. **Act as [expert]:** "Act as a senior [role] with 20 years of experience. Analyze..."
2. **CLEAR Framework:** [C]ontext + [L]ength + [E]xamples + [A]sk + [R]ole
3. **Step-by-step:** "Think through this step by step before answering..."
4. **Format control:** "Return as: numbered list / table / bullet points / JSON"
5. **Iteration:** "Good, now make it: shorter / more formal / more specific about X"
6. **Chain:** "Based on that output, now create..."
7. **Devil's advocate:** "What are the 5 strongest arguments against this?"
8. **Calibrate:** "Rate your confidence in this answer from 1-10 and explain why"
9. **Simplify:** "Explain this like I'm 10, then like I'm an expert"
10. **Verify:** "What sources would you check to verify these claims?"

### Power Moves (Follow-Up Commands)

- **"Continue"** — resumes a cut-off response
- **"Be more specific about [X]"** — drills into any point
- **"Now make a checklist"** — converts any content into action steps
- **"What am I missing?"** — surfaces blindspots
- **"Add concrete examples"** — makes abstract ideas tangible
- **"What are the counterarguments?"** — builds critical thinking
- **"Translate to plain English"** — removes jargon
- **"Now write it for [different audience]"** — adapts tone

### Do NOT Do

- Never share passwords, SSNs, full credit card numbers
- Never paste confidential contracts or trade secrets
- Always fact-check statistics and recent events
- Don't use AI output verbatim for medical or legal decisions
- Don't assume AI has current information (training data has cutoffs)

### Model Selection Guide

| Model | Best For |
|-------|----------|
| **GPT-4o** | Best reasoning, image analysis, complex tasks |
| **GPT-4o mini** | Fast, cheap, great for simple tasks and high volume |
| **o1 / o1-mini** | Deep reasoning, math, logic — slower but more accurate |

## Sheet 2: Claude AI Reference Card

### Claude's Strengths

- **Long documents:** Analyzes up to 200,000 tokens (150,000+ words)
- **Coding:** Writes clean, well-commented code with explanations
- **Analysis:** Nuanced reasoning with multiple perspectives
- **Writing:** Natural, less-robotic output than GPT for creative work
- **Safety:** Better at refusing genuinely harmful requests while being helpful

### Unique Claude Techniques

- **XML tags:** `<task>...</task>` `<context>...</context>` for structured prompts
- **Prefill:** "I'll analyze this in 3 parts:" — Claude continues the pattern
- **Thinking:** Ask "think step by step" for complex problems
- **Persona:** "You are [expert]. Your goal is [X]. User: [question]"
- **Document analysis:** Paste full PDFs (up to 100 pages) for analysis

### Best Use Cases for Claude

- Analyzing long contracts, reports, or research papers
- Complex coding projects with multiple files
- Nuanced ethical or strategic questions requiring balance
- Long-form writing that needs to sound human
- Summarizing and extracting insights from huge documents

### Pricing
- **claude.ai free tier:** Limited daily usage
- **Claude Pro ($20/month):** Priority access, more usage
- **API:** Pay per token

### Subject Starters

### Style Modifiers

- minimalist

### Lighting Options
- golden hour
- studio lighting
- dramatic chiaroscuro
- soft diffused light
- neon glow
- backlit silhouette

### Essential Parameters

| Parameter | What It Does |
|-----------|--------------|
| `--ar 16:9` | Widescreen (YouTube thumbnails, banners) |
| `--ar 9:16` | Vertical (Instagram Reels, TikTok) |
| `--ar 1:1` | Square (Instagram posts) |
| `--ar 4:5` | Portrait (Instagram feed) |
| `--v 6` | Latest model (best quality) |
| `--q 2` | Higher quality (slower, more credits) |
| `--s 750` | Medium stylization (0=natural, 1000=max artistic) |
| `--no [element]` | Exclude something from the image |
| `--chaos 50` | More variation between results (0-100) |
| `--seed [number]` | Reproduce similar results |

## Sheet 4: Universal AI Prompt Formulas

### The 8 Formulas That Work for Any AI Tool

**1. The Expert Ask**
- **Template:** "Act as a [expert type] with [X years] experience in [field]. [Your question or task]."
- **When to use:** Any specialized topic

**2. The Before/After**
- **Template:** "Here is [before: current version]. Rewrite it to be [desired improvement]."
- **When to use:** Improving existing content

**3. The Framework Builder**
- **Template:** "Create a framework/checklist/template for [process]. It should work for [audience] and address [specific challenge]."
- **When to use:** Creating reusable systems

**4. The Socratic Method**
- **Template:** "Ask me 5 questions that will help me clarify my thinking on [topic]. After I answer, give your analysis."
- **When to use:** Clarifying your own thoughts

**5. The Competitor Lens**
- **Template:** "Analyze this from the perspective of [my competitor/skeptic/customer]. What would they say is wrong with this?"
- **When to use:** Stress-testing ideas

**6. The Scenario Planner**
- **Template:** "If [situation], what would the best outcome look like? What steps lead there? What could go wrong?"
- **When to use:** Planning and risk assessment

**7. The Constraint Creator**
- **Template:** "Complete this task with these constraints: [constraint 1], [constraint 2]. Task: [X]."
- **When to use:** Need creative solutions or concise output

**8. The Teach-Back**
- **Template:** "Teach me [topic]. Then ask me 3 questions to test my understanding. Then give me feedback on my answers."
- **When to use:** Actually learning something

## Sheet 7: AI Safety & Ethics Quick Guide

### Never Share These With Public AI Tools

- Social Security / National ID numbers
- Passwords or login credentials
- Full credit/debit card numbers
- Medical records (use general descriptions)
- Legal case details with client names
- Confidential business contracts
- Internal financial data (use approximate numbers)

### Always Verify Independently

- Statistics and data points
- Recent events (training data has cutoffs)
- Medical and legal advice
- Product specifications and prices
- Scientific research claims
- Historical dates and facts in detail

### Workplace Policies Checklist

- ☐ Check your company's AI use policy before using work data in AI
- ☐ Use company-approved AI tools for sensitive projects
- ☐ Never paste customer PII into public AI tools
- ☐ Disclose AI assistance when required by your industry
- ☐ Keep AI-generated code reviewed by humans before production

### Bias Awareness

- AI reflects biases in its training data
- Always consider diverse perspectives on sensitive topics
- AI can confidently be wrong — confidence does not equal accuracy
- Controversial topics may receive politically-influenced responses
- Ask for multiple viewpoints on complex issues

### Quick Safety Checklist Before Sending Any Prompt

1. ☐ Am I sharing any personally identifiable information?
2. ☐ Is this confidential business data?
3. ☐ Will I fact-check any statistics or citations this generates?
4. ☐ Is this for a decision that requires professional advice (legal/medical/financial)?
5. ☐ Does my employer's AI policy allow this use?

If you answered YES to any of 1-4 or NO to 5, reconsider or adjust your prompt.
